QUEUE:
- Queue is based on FIFO (First In First Out).

implementation:
- Queue is not created using array, except circular queue.

operations:
- add() //O(1)
- remove() //O(1)
- peek() //O(1)


DEQUE:
- Double Ended Queue.
- deque operations:
    addFirst()
    addLast()
    removeFirst()
    removeLast()
    getFirst()
    getLast()
