HEAPS:
  a. binary tree:
  - at most 2 children

  b. Complete binary tree:
  - CBT is a BT in which all the levels are completely filled except possibly the last one, which is filled from the left to right.

  c. Heap order property:
  - Children >= Parent (minHeap)
  - Children <= Parent (maxHeap)

Priority Queues:
- sorting whole data structure based on some Priority of one thing.

Priority Queues in JCF:
- add() O(logn)
- remove() O(logn)
- peek() O(1)
