Loops:
  - Loops are the block of codes repeated till the given condition is satisfied
  - Infinite loops are the loops in which the condition is never failed.

Types of loop:
 - while loop
 - do while loop
 - for loop  

For Loop:
  for(initialisation; condition; updation){
    // codes
  }

While loop:
  initialisation;
  while(condition){
    updation;
  }

break statement:
  - 'break' keyword breaks the loop.  

continue statement:
  - 'continue' keyword skips the current iteration and moves to the next iteration.
